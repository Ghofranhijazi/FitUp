Get Started

Welcome to FitUp your trusted destination for easy access to gyms designed specifically for women, while offering comprehensive childcare solutions.

We strive to make your sports experience more comfortable and smooth by connecting you with the nearest gym that suits your needs, with various options to take care of your children whether inside or near the gym. Our goal is to provide an innovative service that combines comfort and quality to save you time and effort, while ensuring the highest levels of evaluation and credibility.

At FitUp we believe that every woman deserves a complete fitness experience without worrying about taking care of her children. We work to achieve the following goals:

  • Providing convenient solutions: Connecting women to the nearest gyms that offer nursery services.
  • Quality assurance: Showcasing highly rated gyms and nurseries that suit all needs.
  • Simplifying the experience: Providing quick and direct booking that saves time and effort.

Our vision: To become the first platform that enables women to achieve their fitness goals easily while ensuring the safe care of their children.
